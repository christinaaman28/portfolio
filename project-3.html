Packaging and Prototyping Lab

Web page redesign

Problem

Create a webpage for the Visual Communication Technology (VCT) Program's Packaging and Prototyping Lab. This webpage will be linked within the VCT Website hosted on the university's site at www.bgsu.edu. The audience for this webpage will be potential students, current students, and industry leaders.

Inspiration

I researched other pages within the BGSU website, but primarily focused on the VCT program pages. To match the look of the website, I read through the BGSU Brand Guidelines and applied them to my design.

Design Solution

I designed an informative, engaging web mockup for the VCT Program's Packaging and Prototyping Lab following BGSU branding guidelines. I used photos from the program as well as alumni quotes in this design, and put ideas for possible copy in the respective sections.
