Stick Season

Typography Poster

Problem

Create a fun typography piece that utilizes a song lyric. The design should include a background image or texture and a vector graphic.

Inspiration

I was heavily inspired by typography-driven posters I found on Pinterest. I was drawn to the playful interaction between san serif and serif fonts, as well as the integration of a vector graphic.

Design Solution

I designed a playful and dynamic poster that utilizes a song lyric from Noah Kahan's Stick Season. The bold sans serif font complements the thinner script font, along with the muted secondary color palette. I created the phone vector art to help visualize the song lyrics. The left-aligned telephone placed with the right-aligned text adds to the balanced composition. The telephone brings movement and guides the viewer's eye throughout the design.
